Filmmaker & Media Producer

Edinburgh / Boston

I'm a final-year student at the University of Edinburgh and an award-winning filmmaker with a passion for storytelling through post-production. My work spans feature-length documentary, client video, short-form content, and live sports production.

As President of Edinburgh University Television (EUTV), I led the station to a record 8 nominations and 6 wins at national awards events, including recognition from the Royal Television Society and NaSTA.

I'm especially drawn to the intersection of sport and media and how stories of competition, culture, and community come to life on screen. My live production experience includes a Production Runner role at the US Open with Van Wagner.
